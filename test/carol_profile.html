      • Tacorea
        $ Mexican, Korean, Latin American
        809 Bush St
        San Francisco, CA 94108
        12/30/2016

        This place does Korean - Mexican fusion so well! The prices are also super fair for San Francisco.

        My friends and I were about to get two The Big Ricardos, Tacorea Tacos (which comes with 4 tacos), and the Tater Tot Nachos, but the guy at the register actually told us that'd be too much food for three people! It's rare to see someone turn down their customer's food requests, but we greatly appreciated that he warned us.

        We ended up getting:
        - The Big Ricardo: 10/10
        - Tacorea Tacos: 9/10
        - Tater Tot Nachos: 10/10

        You definitely have to eat the burrito with a fork and knife! The Big Ricardo is enough for two people to share! My friends and I loved this hole in a wall and we can't wait to be back! We went on a rainy weekday at around 1:30 pm and it was still packed! I would suggest coming here during non-eating times if you want a seat or just get it togo and find some place else to sit!

        Can't wait to have this the next time I'm by Union Square. Been dreaming about it, since I had it last week.

      • Chinese Duck House
        $ Dim Sum, Cantonese
        2960 S Virginia St
        Reno, NV 89502
        12/30/2016
        • 1 check-in

        Not bad for cheap and fast Chinese food.

        The best part about this restaurant is how fast they serve the food! I would recommend getting the duck, the chicken fried rice, and their beef chow fun! My family and I thought the twice cooked pork was only okay.

        Tip: The menu says "green bean with spicy pepper salt" but it's actually (deep?) fried green beans! I loved this dish though!

        I would definitely suggest coming during their Lunch Special (11:00 am - 3:30 pm) because the prices are definitely on the cheap end. Their Dinner Special (3:30 pm - 9:00 pm) is $2 more for everything on the Lunch Special Menu, but it's still really inexpensive!

      • Tasty Pot
        $$ Taiwanese, Bubble Tea, Hot Pot
        2115 Kittredge St
        Berkeley, CA 94704
        12/18/2016
        • 1 check-in

        Oh my goodness, this place was so bad compared to the Newark location. But the service here is amazing! The servers will ask you multiple times about water or about getting more soup in the pot.

        A couple things:
        - My brother and I both got mild and our pots were definitely way spicier than mild ever is. My brother said maybe it's because everyone is on vacation and they had to hire temporary/seasonal workers, but even then.. consistency is key.
        - They got our order a bit wrong. We wanted one rice, three noodles. They brought out two rices, two noodles. Not a super big deal, but still.
        - The biggest problem!!! THE BEEF WAS SO HARD AND CHEWY. There were three hot pots that had beef and all three had huge thick slices that were definitely overcooked as soon as they brought them out. I'm not sure what part of a cow hot pots use for their meat, but they definitely weren't using the good parts. I couldn't even finish my beef, and I LOVE hot pot beef.

        Super disappointed at this location.

      • Din Ding Dumpling House
        $$ Dim Sum, Shanghainese
        3213 Walnut Ave
        Fremont, CA 94538
        12/18/2016
        • 1 check-in

        What's good here is SO good, but everything else is just mediocre. The good dishes are definitely good enough that I'd rate this place 4 stars though.

        Their Pork XLBs are super yummy! There is so much juice in them that it literally filled up the spoon and there was still juice left inside.
        I personally LOVED their Sheng Jian Bao (Pan-Fried Pork Soup Dumplings). Those were also super juicy and the dough part is so crunchy.

        Their pork chow mein was mediocre as was one of their other pork dishes. My parents did like their pork chow mein though because it is very light and fresh tasting, but I don't think it's worth it. If you're coming here, just order the XLB and the Sheng Jian Bao!!

        If you come around 11:20 there's no wait, but by the time 11:40 rolls around you'll be waiting for a long time, so come earlier if you want a seat right away!
